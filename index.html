Attrition by department

Sales and HR run notably hotter than R&D.

Overtime is the single strongest signal

Employees working overtime leave at ~3x the rate of those who don't.

Attrition by job role

Sales Representatives and Lab Technicians are the highest-risk roles.

Tenure curve

Risk is highest in the first 2 years, then drops sharply.

Job satisfaction and work-life balance

Both drive attrition, but work-life balance shows the steeper gradient at the low end.

Dataset: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance (public, 1,470 records, fictional data released by IBM for analytics practice). Analysis and dashboard by Mohammad Faisal · LinkedIn
